A Lot To Swap

A produce swapping community based in Oxfordshire

Why should you get involved?

Reduce Waste

Swap excess you have grown or produced, reducing the amount of food wasted.

Build a Community

Meet new and likeminded people with shared interests, through a positive cause.

Save Money

Swapping saves money as you can trade with people rather than buy from new.

Share Ideas

Share growing and cooking tips within the community.

Cut Down Plastic Use

Swapping homegrown reduces the need for excess packaging.
